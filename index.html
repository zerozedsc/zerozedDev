Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Muhammad Helmi Bin Rozain. 3rd-year BSc student in Information Intelligence Engineering at Toyama University, Japan, with a strong passion for Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI). I'm eager to learn more about these technologies, currently diving into the mathematics and coding behind Generative Pre-trained Transformers (GPT), a type of Large Language Model (LLM) used by tools like ChatGPT. I’m also exploring deep learning with a focus on text-to-image models like Stable Diffusion, which can be run locally.

I have hands-on experience building software, including point-of-sale systems and portfolio websites, as well as creating simple games using Unity and C#. My enthusiasm for robotics, microcontrollers, and IoT has led to active participation in university robotics projects. With a deep interest in Python, I’m driven by a passion for innovation and dedicated to enhancing customer experiences while shaping the digital landscape, one line of code at a time.
